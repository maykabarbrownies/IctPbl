Black Pink is a South korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rose and Lisa. Also know as one of the top of the line performers in the world, ranking No.4 in the whole Kpop industry.Referred to as the "biggest girl group in the world", Blackpink is the most successful Korean girl group internationally. They were the first girl group to top the Billboard Artist 100 and the first Korean girl group to enter and top the Billboard Emerging Artists chart.Blackpink also became the first female Korean act to receive a certification from the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) with their single "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du" (2018), whose music video was the first by a Korean group to surpass one billion and two billion views and is currently the most-viewed by a Korean group on YouTube.
